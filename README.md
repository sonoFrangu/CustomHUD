# 🌅 Beautiful Day Counter

---

## 📝 About
Ever lose track of time while mining deep underground or building your next mega-base? **Beautiful Day Counter** is a lightweight, client-side mod that adds a sleek, vanilla-friendly HUD to the top of your screen. 

Keep yourself perfectly synced with the Minecraft world without ever needing to craft a clock again. With **Beautiful Day Counter**, you'll always know exactly when to head back to safety.

---

## ✨ Main Features

* **🕒 Beautiful Day Counter Clock:** Displays the precise in-game time in **HH:MM** format.
* **📅 Beautiful Day Counter Day Tracker:** Automatically tracks and displays how many days you have survived in your world.
* **🟦 Beautiful Day Counter 24h Bossbar:** An elegant progress bar that fills as the day passes. Midnight is 0%, noon is 50%, and sunset warns you to get to bed!
* **🌈 Beautiful Day Counter Smooth Transitions (v1.1):** Experience fluid, per-second color fading. The **Beautiful Day Counter** bar shifts beautifully between:
    * 🟡 **Dawn (Yellow)**
    * 🔵 **Day (Blue)**
    * 🌸 **Sunset (Pink)**
    * 🟣 **Night (Purple)**
* **⚔️ Beautiful Day Counter Dynamic Icons:** The HUD icon automatically updates based on your gamemode:
    * *Survival Heart | Hardcore Heart | Creative Grass Block | Adventure Sword | Spectator Eye.*
* **🧩 Beautiful Day Counter Smart UI:** Designed for seamless vanilla integration. **Beautiful Day Counter** automatically pushes down to make room for bossbars (like the Ender Dragon or Wither), preventing any UI overlapping.
* **📸 Beautiful Day Counter Stealth Mode:** Fully respects **F1** (Hide HUD) and **F3** (Debug) modes for clean, professional screenshots.
