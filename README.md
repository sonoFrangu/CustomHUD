# 🌅 Beautiful Day Counter

Ever lose track of time while mining deep underground or building your next mega-base? **[Nome Mod]** is a client-side mod that adds a beautiful, vanilla-friendly HUD to the top of your screen, keeping you perfectly synced with the Minecraft world.

### ✨ Features
* **⏰ Real-Time In-Game Clock & Day Counter:** Always know exactly what time it is (HH:MM) and how many days you've survived.
* **🟦 24-Hour Bossbar Tracker:** An elegant blue progress bar that visually fills up as the day passes. Midnight is 0%, noon is 50%, and sunset warns you to get to bed!
* **⚔️ Dynamic Gamemode Icons:** A neat little icon next to the day counter automatically updates based on your gamemode (Survival Heart, Hardcore Heart, Creative Grass Block, Adventure Sword, or Spectator Eye).
* **🧩 Seamless Vanilla Integration:** It looks and feels like it belongs in the base game. Original Minecraft bossbars (like the Ender Dragon or Wither) will automatically push down to make room for your time tracker, preventing any ugly UI overlapping.
* **📸 Screenshot Friendly:** Fully respects the `F1` (hide HUD) and `F3` (debug screen) modes. Your screenshots will stay perfectly clean!

### ⚙️ Technical Info
* **Client-side only:** You can install this on your client and play on any vanilla multiplayer server!
* **Requires:** Fabric API.
